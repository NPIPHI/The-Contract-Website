{% blocktrans %}Welcome to The Contract{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} "The Contract" is a role-playing game that utilizes a round-robin storytelling format where individual sessions tell a self-contained story, and the position of storyteller is rotated between the various members of the group. Because of this, each game will have a slightly different cast of characters, or "High Rollers", which helps to solve the age-old roleplaying problem of absenteeism.

Groups of players are encouraged to develop their own setting and character concepts. There are no preset classes or paradigms in The Contract, yet characters remain balanced and portable thanks to a robust{% endblocktrans %} Powers system.

{% blocktrans %}What is The Contract?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The System allows for flexible and open character design, setting, and dice, allowing you to be anything from a ninja in feudal japan to a cyborg in the year 2200. All the while character portability is maintained so that you can visit other groups of players without balance concerns. {% endblocktrans %}


Dice

{% blocktrans %} The Contract has a detailed dice system and players guide, but players may choose to use their own dice system as well. {% endblocktrans %}

Powers

{% blocktrans %} Snowflakes of Carnage. The Powers system allows players to fufill nearly any supernatural character concept while playing alongside characters from around the world. {% endblocktrans %}

Format

{% blocktrans %} Each game begins with a powerful individual (known as a Harbinger) inviting a group of High Rollers on a deadly mission where they risk life and limb for a chance to obtain supernatural abilities and change the world. {% endblocktrans %}
